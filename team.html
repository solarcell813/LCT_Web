Biography

Lin received his B.Sc. from National Cheng Kung University in Taiwan, with exchange programs at Temple University (U.S.) and Osaka University (Japan). He completed his MRes and Ph.D. under Prof. Martyn McLachlan and Prof. James Durrant at Imperial College London, focusing on perovskite solar cell stability and photophysical characterisation. He subsequently worked on stable organic photocathodes as a Research Associate at ICL, before moving to GIST (Korea) to develop large-scale perovskite and organic solar cells. He is now an Assistant Professor at NCHU.
