What We Study

Field of Study

🔮 Perovskite Research Photo
Perovskite · ABX₃

Perovskite Solar Cells

Engineering ABX₃ perovskite absorbers from single-crystal growth to large-area thin-film deposition, targeting the efficiency limits and long-term operational stability essential for commercial deployment.

  • Single-crystal perovskite growth & characterisation
  • Thin-film stability under thermal, humidity & light stress
  • Interface passivation & charge-transport optimisation
  • Large-area blade-coating and vacuum crystallisation
🧬 Organic PV Research Photo
Organic PV · D:A

Organic Photovoltaics

Designing all-polymer donor–acceptor systems for mechanically robust, solution-processable solar cells, with a focus on morphology control, photophysical characterisation, and scalable device fabrication.

  • All-polymer donor–acceptor blend optimisation
  • Morphology control for high-efficiency OPV
  • Quasi-Fermi level splitting & QFLS mapping
  • Organic photocathodes for solar-driven HER
